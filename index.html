“Ek Tarfa Chahat” – Part 1 (Deep Version)

🌙 Zindagi ek ajeeb silsila hai…
jo hum chahte hain, zaruri nahi hamesha humein mile,
aur jo humein milta hai, zaruri nahi wo humari chahat ho.
Par sabse khoobsurat ehsaas tab paida hota hai,
jab hum apni chahat ko uski khushi ke liye qurbaan kar dete hain.

✨ Log kehte hain ek tarfa mohabbat khoobsurat hoti hai…
Haan, hoti hai.
Par ek raaz hai—
ek tarfa chahat usse bhi zyada khoobsurat hoti hai.

💫 Mohabbat mein hum mangte hain—
ki wo bhi humein chahe, humein paaye, humari ho jaye…
Par chahat mein sirf ek dua hoti hai—
ki wo hamesha muskuraye,
chahe uski muskaan ke peeche humara dard kyun na chhup jaye.

🌌 Ek tarfa mohabbat mein apne armaan hote hain,
par ek tarfa chahat mein sirf uski khushi hoti hai.
Aur yahi farq usse paak banata hai.

🌺 Socho…
jab tum usse mile the, tumhe khud nahi pata tha
ki ek din tumhara dil uske liye dhadkega.
To phir aaj kyun dard ho raha hai
agar wo kisi aur ke saath khush hai?
Tumhari chahat to uski khushi thi na…
phir chahe wo tumse judi ho, ya tumse juda.

🌷 Sachi chahat wahi hai—
jo chup rahe, par kabhi kam na ho.
Jo apne jazbaat ka izhar na kare,
sirf duaon mein uska zikr kare.
Aur jab zarurat pade,
to apne dil ke tukde kar ke bhi,
uske raaste mein phool bicha de.

🌒 Kabhi kabhi kuch jazbaat chhupane padte hain…
kyunki kuch sachaiyan izhar se zyada,
khamoshi mein khoobsurat lagti hain.
Aur wahi khamoshi, ek tarfa chahat ko
ek adhoori si, par sabse behtareen dastaan banati hai.

🌙 Ye sirf Part 1 hai…
Agla part tabhi aayega,
jab dil phir se lafzon ke bojh se halka hona chahega,
aur khud se baat karne ke liye kagaz ka sahara lega.
Tab tak… ek shukriya,
ki tumne meri is adhoori si kahani ke pehle panne ko mehsoos kiya. ✨


BY:- Akshat Singh🥀✍️
